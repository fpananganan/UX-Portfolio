Modern design meets minimalist -- the application offers a clean and simple user experience for content creators to collect, collaborate, create and share their work with other creatives and fellow followers.

Problem

To date, there are several cloud storage platforms available to consumers, most of which try to cover a wide audience.

What about a niche crowd of content creators? How could a platform offer all in one services for organization, content creation and collaboration?

Solution

ARCHETYPE aims to solve that problem by streamlining these services into one main platform. It will allow users to create and organize content, collaborate with other creatives, and share their work with their followers.
